# Super Shop Performance Dashboard 
# Project Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail performance across sales, profitability, and customer behavior, enabling data-driven business decisions.

# Executive Summary
The business demonstrates strong growth, with revenue increasing by 13.1% and profit by 13.0% month-over-month, indicating sustained expansion and healthy performance.
Store A contributes the highest revenue ($4,933), making it the primary performance driver, while the Dairy category generates the highest profit ($1,779), highlighting category-level profitability strength.
Customer purchasing behavior remains stable, with an average of 6 items per order, supporting consistent sales volume. However, a slight decline in profit margin (-0.1%) suggests emerging cost pressures and an opportunity for margin optimization

# Business Questions
1.	How is the business performing over time?
2.	What drives revenue and profit?
3.	How do customers buy?

# Key Insights
1.Revenue and profit grew strongly (+13.1% and +13.0% MoM), indicating consistent business expansion.
2.Store A leads revenue generation ($4,933), making it the key contributor to overall performance.
3. Dairy is the most profitable category ($1,779 profit), highlighting its importance in profit optimization.
4. Profit margin slightly declined (-0.1%), suggesting rising costs or pricing pressure.
5. Customer behavior is stable (6 items per order), supporting predictable demand patterns.

# Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:
1.Strengthen high-performing stores
Since Store A leads revenue ($4,933), replicate its successful strategies (product mix, pricing, promotions) across other stores to improve overall performance.
2. Expand high-profit categories
The Dairy category generates the highest profit ($1,779), indicating an opportunity to increase inventory, promotions, and visibility in this segment.
3. Address margin pressure
A -0.1% decline in profit margin suggests rising costs or pricing inefficiencies. Review supplier costs and optimize pricing strategies to protect profitability.
4.Leverage stable customer behavior
With consistent purchasing patterns (6 items per order), introduce bundle offers and upselling strategies to increase average order value.
5. Optimize payment experience
Analyze payment method distribution to promote faster or lower-cost payment channels, improving operational efficiency and customer experience

# Business Impact
* Identifies top-performing stores and products for strategic focus
*	Highlights profitability drivers and margin risks
*	Supports pricing and cost optimization decisions
*	Enables better understanding of customer purchasing patterns
