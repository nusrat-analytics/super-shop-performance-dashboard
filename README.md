# Super Shop Performance Dashboard 

# Project Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail performance across sales, profitability, and customer behavior, enabling data-driven business decisions.

# Executive Summary
The business demonstrates strong growth, with revenue increasing by 13.1% and profit by 13.0% month-over-month, indicating sustained expansion and healthy performance.
Store A contributes the highest revenue ($4,933), making it the primary performance driver, while the Dairy category generates the highest profit ($1,779), highlighting category-level profitability strength.
Customer purchasing behavior remains stable, with an average of 6 items per order, supporting consistent sales volume. However, a slight decline in profit margin (-0.1%) suggests emerging cost pressures and an opportunity for margin optimization
